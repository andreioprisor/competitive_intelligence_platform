## Your Mission

1. **Where we lead**: Features/capabilities where we're stronger

## Research Strategy

### Primary Research Sources
1. **Direct product comparison**:
   - Official product pages (ours vs. theirs)
   - Feature lists and capability matrices
   - Pricing pages (ours vs. theirs)

2. **Third-party comparisons**:
   - G2 / Capterra / TrustRadius comparison pages
   - "[our company] vs [competitor]" articles
   - Analyst reports with feature comparisons
   - Review sites with side-by-side comparisons

3. **Customer evidence**:
   - Reviews mentioning both products
   - Case studies and testimonials
   - Forum discussions comparing the two
   - Reddit, Twitter, LinkedIn conversations
